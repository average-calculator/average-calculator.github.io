EMI Calculator

An EMI Calculator (Equated Monthly Installment Calculator) is a crucial financial tool designed to calculate the fixed monthly payment amount required to repay a loan over a specified tenure at a given interest rate. Whether you're considering a car loan or a home loan, an EMI calculator helps borrowers worldwide thoroughly understand their repayment obligations and effectively plan their finances.

To use our comprehensive EMI calculation tool, simply enter the loan amount (principal), the annual interest rate (percentage), and the loan tenure (expressed in months) into the respective fields below. Then, click the "Calculate EMI" button. The calculator will instantly compute the EMI amount, the total interest payable throughout the loan term, and the total payment (which includes both the principal loan amount and the total interest). The results will be clearly displayed, empowering you to make informed financial decisions regarding your loans.
